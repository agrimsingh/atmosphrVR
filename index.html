VIRTUAL REALITY VISUALIZED

With Atmosphr, we provide a software platform to enable the creation of VR concepts for real-world applications.

We realize that the current suite of software is tedious for the end-user, and thus bringing ideas to fruition is hard, especially without visualization that allows you to see what you actually design.

EASY AS 1-2-3

Get started right away using our curated assets.

INTUITIVE USER INTERFACE

It feels so natural! Drag your preferred items into the environment just like a jigsaw puzzle. No coding or background knowledge required!

CUSTOMIZABLE

Choose a room that meets your needs. None of them do? Upload your own floor plan and get started!

EXPANDABLE

Are you a 3D designer or use custom 3D assets? You can upload your own designs into the environment as well! If you're a company interested in having your catalogue on our platform, get in touch!

SEAMLESS

No extra apps to download. Simply open our generated link on your phone, slide it into the VR headset and you're all set.

WHO IS THIS FOR?

VR is going to shape every aspect of our lives in ways unimaginable. Accessible VR and the ability to experience it first-hand is the first step towards realizing how this will shape up.

INTERIOR DECORATORS

Create a virtual instance of a house right in the browser and allow users to experience their homes first-hand.

EVENT PLANNERS

Need to try different layouts for your party or a corporate event? Stop planning on paper and visualize your plans in a jiffy. Our product brings your concept to life before you roll it out in the real world.

ADVERTISERS

Use our product to plan out your campaigns - think A/B testing, user engagement and more!

USERS

Find a use case that hits home! Perhaps it's planning your classroom layout as a teacher or room layouts at hospitals. Just like 'The Sims', Atmosphr has you covered.
